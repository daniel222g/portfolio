Identity for Thetfest
Thetfest was an annual 2-day music festival held in rural Thetford, Vermont. I designed this prospective identity for the festival, which includes a large format poster, wristbands, and a downloadable schedule. I created a handcrafted aesthetic somewhere between hippie and punk, evoking the vibe of the festival. (24 × 47 ½" poster)
